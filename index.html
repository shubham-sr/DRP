DISTRIBUTOR REGISTRATION PROCESS

Steps for registering as a Distributor/Business Partner


  • Click the ‘DRF’ option from the menu bar of the site.
  • Now you can dowload or print the form after clicking on DRF.
  • Fill the form physically as instructions given in that.
  • Scan the following : (a) Fully filled Registration Form (b) Aadhar Card Copy (c) Pan Card Copy (d) Aadhar Card Copy of Nominee (e) Bank Passbook
  • Send these uploaded documents to our mail address, contact@hitsbig.com

After verification, we will send your distributor ID as well as your DID Card to your registered mail address.
